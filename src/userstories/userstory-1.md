# Example Investigator: A User Story

Example user story for an investigator at requesting institutions, with a research objective that will require non-trivial data linkage from multiple datasets at different institutions. 

- What are the challenges for the investigator?
- Where are the opportunities to streamline the process?
- How would the LGF make life easier for the investigator?
- What are the benefits of the common governance process?
- How does this benefit the research proposed by the investigator?

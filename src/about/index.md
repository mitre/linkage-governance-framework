# About the Linkage Governance Framework

The Linkage Governance Framework is part of a [larger project](https://aspe.hhs.gov/creating-new-data-research-environment) funded through the Office of the Secretary Patient-Centered Outcomes Research Trust Fund (OS-PCORTF) to the National Cancer Institute (NCI). The OS-PCORTF is charged with building data capacity for patient-centered outcomes research. Coordinated by the Office of the Assistant Secretary for Planning and Evaluation (ASPE) on behalf of the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), the OS-PCORTF has funded a rich portfolio of projects to meet emerging HHS policy priorities and fill gaps in data infrastructure to enhance capabilities to collect, link, and analyze data for patient-centered outcomes research.
